# Where to get help

Diplomat-ai is solo-maintained. Ping the right channel and you'll
get a faster, better answer.

## Questions and discussions

- **GitHub Discussions** on the relevant repo
  ([diplomat-agent](https://github.com/Diplomat-ai/diplomat-agent/discussions),
  [diplomat-gate](https://github.com/Diplomat-ai/diplomat-gate/discussions))
  — design questions, architecture, "is this in scope", policy ideas.
  Searchable. Public. Default channel.

## Bugs and feature requests

- **GitHub Issues** on the relevant repo. Use the templates. A bug
  report without version + Python + OS + reproducer will be closed
  with a request for those.

## Security vulnerabilities

- **Do not** open a public issue. See [`SECURITY.md`](./SECURITY.md).
  Email **security@diplomat.run**.

## Commercial / hosted control plane

- **diplomat.run** — for teams that need cross-tenant audit, real-time
  dashboard, managed approvals, and EU AI Act Article 12 export. Email
  **josselin@diplomat.run** or visit [diplomat.run](https://diplomat.run).

## Response time

- Issues and Discussions: within 5 business days, usually faster.
- Security reports: within 72 hours.
- Email (commercial): within 2 business days.
